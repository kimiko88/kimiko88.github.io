Frequently Asked Questions

Why is the layout missing some advanced functions?
In our latest update, we streamlined the interface to focus on the most commonly used tools based on user feedback. We removed visual clutter (like the graph and hyperbolic features) to maximize speed and usability for daily calculations.
How large can the numbers be?
Thanks to our custom computational engine, there is no practical limit to the size of the numbers you can calculate. You will not see the typical "Error" messages associated with standard calculators.
Are the scientific functions completely accurate?
Yes! We use advanced mathematical Taylor series expansions specifically optimized for iOS. This guarantees extremely high precision for trigonometric functions (Sin, Cos, Tan) and logarithms, even in complex edge cases.
The app crashed or froze. What should I do?
Please ensure you are updated to the latest version via the App Store. If the problem persists, use the "Contact Support" button above and provide details about the calculation you were trying to perform.
