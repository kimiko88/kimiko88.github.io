Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: The spaceship doesn't move correctly or goes in the wrong direction.
A: Use the "Setup" button in the menu and select "Calibrate" while holding your phone in the position you find most comfortable to play.

Q: How do I recover my high scores?
A: Ensure you are logged into Game Center in your iPhone or iPad settings. High scores are automatically saved online.

Q: Purchases are not working or unlocking.
A: Make sure you have an active internet connection and enough Gems in the game. If you have issues with In-App Purchases, try restarting the application.
