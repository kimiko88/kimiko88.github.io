Third-Party Services (Google AdMob)

Unlimited Calculator uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect certain device information to personalize the ads you see or to analyze traffic. The information collected by AdMob may include:

  • Device identifiers (such as Apple's Advertising Identifier - IDFA).
  • IP Addresses.
  • Usage data, cookies, and other tracking technologies.
  • General diagnostic data (like crash reports).

If you have given consent to App Tracking Transparency (ATT) on your iOS device, AdMob might use this data to deliver personalized ads. If you denied consent, AdMob will still serve ads, but they will not be personalized based on your activity across other apps or websites.

For more details on how Google handles your data, please review the Google Privacy Policy.
